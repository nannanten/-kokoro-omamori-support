Privacy Policy

Kokoro Omamori

This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in “Kokoro Omamori” (the “App”).

1Information Stored by the App

The App stores the following information entered or set by the user within the App:

  • Anxiety level
  • Symptoms
  • Triggers
  • Notes
  • Images set for the Omamori card
  • Custom messages set for the Omamori card
  • Notification settings

The App is not designed to request information that directly identifies an individual, such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

However, if the user enters personal information in notes or other fields, that information will be stored as part of the App’s record data on the user’s device.

2Purpose of Use

The information stored in the App is used to allow users to record and review their anxiety, feelings, symptoms, triggers, notes, Omamori card, and notification settings.

The records in the App are intended for personal self-care and reflection only. They are not intended for medical diagnosis, treatment, prevention, or professional advice.

3Storage Location

Information entered or set in the App is stored on the user’s device.

The developer does not transmit, collect, or store the user’s anxiety level, symptoms, triggers, notes, Omamori card image, custom messages, or other record data on an external server.

4Advertising

The App may use third-party advertising services to display ads.

Advertising services may collect and use information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and app usage information for purposes including ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and showing ads that may be more relevant to the user.

Such information is handled in accordance with the privacy policies of the advertising service providers.

5Managing Advertising Information

Users may be able to reset their advertising identifier or change settings related to personalized advertising from their device settings.

On iOS devices, users can review and change tracking and advertising-related settings from the privacy-related settings in the device’s Settings app.

Available settings and menu names may differ depending on the OS version and device status.

6Sharing with Third Parties

The developer does not provide record data entered or set by users in the App to third parties, except where required by law.

However, if the App uses advertising services, the advertising service providers may collect and use information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and app usage information for ad delivery and measurement purposes.

7Notifications

The App may send reminder notifications if the user chooses to enable them.

Notification settings can be changed or turned off from the App settings or the device settings.

8Use of Images and Custom Messages

The App allows users to set an image and a custom message for their Omamori card.

The selected image and custom message are used only within the App on the user’s device. The developer does not collect, view, or access them.

9Deleting Data

Users can delete saved record data from the settings screen in the App.

If the App is deleted from the device, data stored within the App may also be deleted.

10Purpose and Limitations of the App

The App is intended as a record-keeping and support tool that helps users reflect on their feelings and anxiety as part of personal self-care.

The App does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medical care, counseling, or professional advice by doctors, clinical psychologists, licensed psychologists, or other professionals.

The content displayed in the App, record results, reflection content, breathing guide, Omamori card messages, and other features do not guarantee improvement in mental or physical condition, reduction of symptoms, or any specific result.

If you have physical or mental health concerns, strong anxiety, worsening symptoms, thoughts of self-harm, suicidal thoughts, or any urgent condition, do not rely on this App alone. Please consult a medical institution, qualified professional, someone close to you, or an emergency support service.
