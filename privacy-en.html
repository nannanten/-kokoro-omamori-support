Privacy Policy

Kokoro Omamori

This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in “Kokoro Omamori” (the “App”).

1Information Stored by the App

The App stores the following information entered by the user within the App:

  • Anxiety level
  • Symptoms
  • Triggers
  • Notes
  • Images set for the Omamori card
  • Notification settings

The App is not intended to ask users to enter information that directly identifies them, such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

2Purpose of Use

The stored information is used so that users can record and review their daily anxiety and feelings.

The records in the App are not intended to provide medical treatment, diagnosis, or professional advice.

3Storage Location

The records entered in the App are stored on the user’s device.

The developer does not send or collect the user’s record data to an external server.

4Sharing with Third Parties

The developer does not provide information entered by users in the App to third parties, except where required by law.

5Notifications

The App may send reminder notifications if the user chooses to enable them.

Notification settings can be changed or turned off from the App settings or the device settings.

6Use of Images

The App allows users to set an image for their Omamori card.

The selected image is used only on the user’s device. The developer does not collect, view, or access the image.

7Deleting Data

Users can delete saved record data from the settings screen in the App.

If the App is deleted from the device, data stored within the App may also be deleted.

8Disclaimer

The App is intended to support self-care and personal record keeping.

The App does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice by a physician or other professional. If you have physical or mental health concerns, or if you are experiencing strong anxiety, please consult a medical institution or qualified professional.

If you are in immediate danger or feel that you may harm yourself, do not rely on this App alone. Please contact someone close to you, a medical institution, or an emergency support service immediately.

9Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.

Any changes will become effective when posted within the App or on the App Store.

10Contact

For inquiries about the App, please contact us through the contact information listed on the App Store.
