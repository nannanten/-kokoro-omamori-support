Privacy Policy

Kokoro Omamori

This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in “Kokoro Omamori” (the “App”).

1Information Stored by the App

The App stores the following information entered or set by the user within the App:

  • Anxiety level
  • Symptoms
  • Triggers
  • Notes
  • Images set for the Omamori card
  • Custom messages set for the Omamori card
  • Notification settings

The App is not designed to request information that directly identifies an individual, such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

2Purpose of Use

The information stored in the App is used to allow users to record and review their anxiety, feelings, symptoms, triggers, notes, Omamori card, and notification settings.

The records in the App are intended for personal self-care and reflection only. They are not intended for medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional advice.

3Storage Location

Information entered or set in the App is stored on the user’s device.

The developer does not transmit, collect, or store the user’s record data on an external server.

4Sharing with Third Parties

The developer does not provide information entered or set by users in the App to third parties, except where required by law.

5Notifications

The App may send reminder notifications if the user chooses to enable them.

Notification settings can be changed or turned off from the App settings or the device settings.

6Use of Images and Custom Messages

The App allows users to set an image and a custom message for their Omamori card.

The selected image and custom message are used only within the App on the user’s device. The developer does not collect, view, or access them.

7Deleting Data

Users can delete saved record data from the settings screen in the App.

If the App is deleted from the device, data stored within the App may also be deleted.

8Disclaimer

The App is intended to support self-care and personal record keeping.

The App does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, counseling, or any other professional service. If you have physical or mental health concerns, or if you are experiencing strong anxiety or distress, please consult a medical institution, counselor, or other qualified professional.

The developer is not responsible for any damage or disadvantage arising from the use of the App, except in cases of intentional misconduct or gross negligence by the developer.

If you are in immediate danger or feel that you may harm yourself, do not rely on this App alone. Please contact someone close to you, a medical institution, an emergency service, or a local crisis support service immediately.

9Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.

Any changes will become effective when posted within the App, on the App Store, or on the support page for the App.
